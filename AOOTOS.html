Terms of Service (TOS)

1. Membership Commitment

  • By joining the AOO, members agree to uphold the principles of order, respect, and confidentiality.
  • Members must actively participate in assigned roles and duties.

2. Conduct

  • Members must maintain a high standard of conduct, both within and outside of AOO activities.
  • Any behavior that disrupts order or violates AOO principles may result in disciplinary action or removal.

3. Confidentiality

  • Information shared within the AOO, especially regarding undercover operations, must remain confidential.
  • Breach of confidentiality will lead to immediate consequences.

4. Role Responsibilities

  • All members are expected to fulfill their designated roles as outlined by the AOO leadership.
  • Failure to meet role expectations may lead to reassignment or removal from the role.

5. Conflict Resolution

  • Any disputes within the AOO will be resolved internally, with the involvement of founders or headmasters if necessary.
  • Physical altercations or disruptive behavior are strictly prohibited.

6. Termination of Membership

  • Membership can be voluntarily terminated at any time by notifying AOO leadership.
  • The AOO reserves the right to revoke membership for any violations of these terms.

7. Amendments

  • The AOO leadership reserves the right to amend these terms at any time, with notice provided to all members.
